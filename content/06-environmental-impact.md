## 6.1 Embedded carbon avoided through laptop refurbishment

- Average embedded CO₂ for a new business laptop: ~200–300 kg CO₂eq (cite Labdoo / eReuse / academic LCA studies).
- Multiplied by 9 refurbished units in this deployment → tonnes of CO₂ avoided.
- Extension of useful life: from ~5 yr corporate lifecycle to ~8–10 yr community lifecycle.
